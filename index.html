About

I'm passionate about creating solutions with the use of technology to help ease the problems that we face. I'm a self-taught programmer and a designer; I have been coding for about 3 years, mostly on iOS (Swift, NodeJS). I have written several other apps in Swift with native technologies as well as various third-party libraries and API integrations with Cocoapods. I also have a passion for designing and have designed several user interfaces.

Recently, I have gained tremendous interest in machine learning and artificial intelligence, and I'm learning the language, python. I believe that there are so many untapped opportunities in ML and AI. I believe it has the ability to change how we tackle problems in the future.

Other than coding, I love reading, painting, playing/learning keyboard, and learning about astrophysics.
